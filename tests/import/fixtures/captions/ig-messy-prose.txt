okay so everyone keeps asking for my grandma's lemon ricotta pancakes and i'm finally writing it down lol. you need a cup and a half of flour, a cup of whole milk ricotta (don't use the fat free stuff trust me), 2 eggs, 3/4 cup milk, zest of 2 lemons plus the juice of one, 3 tablespoons sugar, 2 teaspoons baking powder and a pinch of salt. whisk the dry stuff together first, then in another bowl beat the eggs with the ricotta, milk, lemon zest and juice. fold it all together but DON'T overmix, lumps are fine!! butter a pan on medium low and do about 1/4 cup per pancake, flip when bubbly, like 3 min a side. we eat them with honey and berries. my grandma would put a tiny bit of vanilla in too if she was feeling fancy ✨
