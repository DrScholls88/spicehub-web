CRISPY CHILI GARLIC NOODLES 🍜🔥 the 10-minute dinner that broke my DMs

INGREDIENTS:
• 8 oz wide rice noodles
• 3 tbsp neutral oil
• 6 cloves garlic, minced
• 1 tbsp chili crisp (more if you're brave)
• 2 tbsp soy sauce
• 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
• 1 tsp rice vinegar
• 1 tsp brown sugar
• 2 green onions, sliced
• Toasted sesame seeds

DIRECTIONS:
1. Cook noodles according to package, drain and rinse with cold water.
2. Heat oil in a wok over medium. Add garlic and fry until just golden, about 90 seconds.
3. Stir in chili crisp, both soy sauces, vinegar and sugar.
4. Toss in noodles and crank heat to high. Toss 2 minutes until edges crisp.
5. Top with green onions and sesame seeds. Serve immediately.

NOTES: Gluten free if you use tamari! Keeps 3 days in the fridge.
