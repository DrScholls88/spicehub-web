Brown Butter Miso Cookies 🍪 chewy edges, fudgy middles

Ingredients:
1 cup unsalted butter
2¼ cups all-purpose flour
½ tsp baking soda
¾ cup dark brown sugar, packed
⅓ cup granulated sugar
2 tbsp white miso paste
1 egg + 1 yolk
1½ tsp vanilla extract
⅔ cup chopped dark chocolate
Flaky salt for topping

Method:
Brown the butter in a saucepan until nutty and amber, then cool 20 minutes.
Whisk flour and baking soda together.
Beat the cooled brown butter with both sugars and the miso for 2 minutes.
Add egg, yolk and vanilla, then mix in the dry ingredients.
Fold in the chocolate. Chill the dough at least 1 hour.
Scoop onto lined sheets and bake at 350°F for 11–12 minutes.
Top with flaky salt while warm.
