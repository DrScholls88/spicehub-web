Smoked Paloma Negra 🥃 my new favorite agave cocktail

2 oz mezcal
0.75 oz fresh lime juice
0.5 oz agave syrup
2 oz grapefruit soda
1 dash saline solution
Tajín for the rim

Rim a highball glass with Tajín. Shake the mezcal, lime, agave and saline with ice. Strain into the glass over fresh ice. Top with grapefruit soda and give it one gentle stir. Garnish with a grapefruit wedge.
