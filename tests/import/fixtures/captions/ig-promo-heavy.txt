What you need:
1 cup self-rising flour
1 cup non-fat greek yogurt
1 egg, beaten
Everything bagel seasoning

How to:
Preheat oven to 375F.
Mix flour and yogurt until a shaggy dough forms, then knead 2 minutes.
Divide into 4, roll into ropes and pinch into rings.
Brush with egg and coat generously with seasoning.
Bake 23 minutes until deep golden.
