Smash Burger Tacos (Big Mac Tacos)

Crispy-edged beef smashed onto tortillas with special sauce.
Servings: 6
Total Time: 25 minutes

Tacos

  • 1.5 lbs 80/20 ground beef
  • 6 small flour tortillas
  • 6 slices American cheese
  • Shredded iceberg lettuce
  • 1/2 white onion, minced
  • Dill pickle chips

Special Sauce

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp yellow mustard
  • 2 tbsp sweet pickle relish
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • Stir all special sauce ingredients together and refrigerate.
  • Divide beef into 6 balls. Press one ball thinly across each tortilla, edge to edge.
  • Place beef-side down on a hot griddle and smash flat. Cook 2-3 minutes until crusty.
  • Flip, top with cheese, and cook 1 more minute until the tortilla crisps.
  • Dress with sauce, lettuce, onion and pickles. Fold and eat immediately.

Notes

Don't salt the beef until it hits the griddle or the patties turn springy.
