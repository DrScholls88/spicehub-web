Whipped Feta Flatbreads with Hot Honey

Ten minutes, one food processor, zero regrets. This is my most-requested appetizer of 2026 so far.

Whipped Feta Flatbreads with Hot Honey

4 flatbreads · 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 8 oz block feta, drained
  • 4 oz cream cheese, room temperature
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil, plus more for brushing
  • 1 small garlic clove
  • 4 store-bought naan or flatbreads
  • 2 tbsp hot honey, plus more to serve
  • 1/4 cup pistachios, roughly chopped
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Aleppo pepper and flaky salt

Instructions

  1. Blitz feta, cream cheese, olive oil and garlic in a food processor for 2 minutes until completely smooth, scraping down once.
  2. Brush flatbreads with oil and warm in a 425F oven or on a grill pan until blistered, about 4 minutes.
  3. Swoosh a thick layer of whipped feta over each flatbread.
  4. Drizzle with hot honey and scatter pistachios, mint, Aleppo and flaky salt over the top.
  5. Slice and serve warm.

Notes

Whipped feta keeps 4 days refrigerated; re-whip with a splash of oil.
