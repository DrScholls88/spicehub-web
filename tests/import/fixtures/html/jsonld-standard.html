One-Pan Lemon Herb Chicken Orzo

By Marisol Beaumont-Tran · Published March 14, 2026 · Updated June 2, 2026

If February had a flavor it would be this skillet: golden chicken thighs, orzo that drinks up every bit of lemony broth, and enough feta to make it feel indulgent on a Tuesday.

My husband Ossian — famously indifferent to "one-pan anything" — asked for this three times in one week. That never happens.

A few reader questions from last week: yes, boneless thighs work (drop the covered simmer to 11 minutes). No, arborio is not a good swap for orzo here.
