Nana's Sunday Gravy

Four hours, three meats, one pot. The sauce that raised us.

8 servings
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lb sweet Italian sausage links
  • 1 lb beef braciole or chuck, cut in chunks
  • 1 lb pork spare ribs
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 5 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 2 (28 oz) cans San Marzano tomatoes, crushed by hand
  • 1 (6 oz) can tomato paste
  • 1 cup dry red wine
  • Handful of fresh basil
  • 1 rind of Parmigiano-Reggiano

Brown the sausage, beef and ribs in batches in the oil; set aside. Soften the onion, add garlic and tomato paste and fry until brick red. Deglaze with wine. Add tomatoes, basil, cheese rind and the browned meats with their juices. Simmer partially covered on the lowest heat for 4 hours, stirring every 30 minutes. Skim the fat, season, and serve over rigatoni with the meats on a platter.
