What I cooked this week (vol. 47)

Monday was leftovers night, which in this house means "fridge risotto" — whatever vegetables are wilting, softened in butter, plus arborio and stock until it surrenders.

Wednesday I finally tried the intersection of two obsessions: I folded kimchi into a grilled cheese. Verdict: life-changing, ten out of ten, would betray my heritage again.

The weekend was for a slow braise — no measurements, just instinct, a bottle of wine that was too good for cooking, and four hours of the house smelling like somebody loves you.

No recipe cards this week. Sometimes cooking is just paying attention.
