# 🤍 Marry Me Creamy Tofu Dump & Bake 💚

**Source:** @easyveganwithsam on Instagram
**Posted:** December 23, 2025

---

## Recipe Overview

This is rich, creamy, cozy, and ridiculously easy - you just dump everything into one dish and let the oven do the work 😍

**Dietary Tags:** Gluten-Free • Vegan • One Pan

---

## Ingredients ✨

- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp Italian seasoning
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp paprika
- ½ cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
- 1 cup zucchini/bell pepper, chopped
- 1 block extra-firm tofu, cubed
- 4 cups regular/gluten-free pasta (penne, rigatoni, or macaroni)
- 2½ cups vegetable broth
- 1 can coconut milk
- 2 handfuls baby spinach
- Salt & pepper, to taste
- 1 cup vegan mozzarella (optional but amazing)
- 1 tsp chili flakes (optional)

---

## Directions 👩‍🍳

1. **Prep:** Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).

2. **Combine:** Add everything except the spinach and vegan mozzarella to a large baking dish. Stir well so the pasta and tofu are fully coated.

3. **First Bake:** Cover tightly with foil/lid and bake for 35–45 minutes, until the pasta is tender and most of the liquid is absorbed.

4. **Add Spinach & Cheese:** Remove from the oven, stir in the spinach, and sprinkle the vegan mozzarella on top.

5. **Final Bake:** Return to the oven uncovered for 5–10 minutes, until golden and bubbly.

6. **Serve:** Enjoy!!! 😉
